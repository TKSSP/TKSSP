About the Society

Our Mission

"Uniting Telugu artists in Pune, one performance at a time."

Telugu Kala Samskruti Society, Pune is a registered cultural organisation dedicated to creating and providing a platform for Telugu artists residing in Pune.

We actively conduct events and performances throughout the year — showcasing the rich tapestry of Telugu performing arts, visual arts, literature, and culture across Maharashtra and beyond.

The society bridges the vibrant Telugu community in Pune with the broader cultural landscape of India, preserving heritage while nurturing new generations of artists.
